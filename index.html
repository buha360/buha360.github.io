What Data We Collect

GPS Location Data

At MapCanva, we prioritize your experience and privacy. Our app is designed to use GPS data solely for enhancing your interaction with the map features. When you launch MapCanva and if the GPS function is enabled on your device, we use your location data to automatically zoom in to your current location on the map. This feature is intended to provide you with a seamless and personalized map drawing experience, allowing you to start creating right where you are. We want to assure you that the use of GPS data is completely under your control. If the GPS function is not enabled when you start the app, we won't nag you to turn it on. Instead, the app will start with a default view over Europe, and you can manually navigate to your desired location. This approach ensures that you are not compelled to share your location if you prefer not to; however, it means that the automatic zoom feature will not be available.

Privacy by Design

After the initial location is used to zoom in to your position, we immediately remove the location indicator icon from the map. This step is taken to prevent any accidental leakage of your exact whereabouts, further safeguarding your privacy. It's important for us to clarify that MapCanva does not collect, store, or use your GPS location data when the app is closed or not in use. Moreover, we do not share your location data with third parties for any purpose. Our commitment to privacy extends beyond the active use of MapCanva. We ensure that no location data is gathered or retained when the app is running in the background or when you're not actively using it. Your privacy and the security of your data are paramount, and we design our features with this in mind.
